About

Hello Venus (헬로비너스) currently consists of 6 members: Alice, Nara, Lime, Seoyoung, Yooyoung, and Yeoreum. The band debuted on 2012, under Tricell Media (a joint between Pledis Entertainment and Fantagio). In 2014, it was announced that the 2 company split and Hello Venus will continue under Fantagio. They disbanded on April 18, 2019.
